# California Department of Social Services (CDSS) Public Records Request
**Sent Via:** CDSS PRA Portal / Email (PRA@dss.ca.gov)
**Date:** July 1, 2026

**RE: Public Records Act Request — Orange County Child Welfare Placements by Zip Code**

Dear Public Records Officer,

Pursuant to the California Public Records Act (Gov. Code § 7920.000 et seq.), I am writing to request copies of public records concerning child welfare placements in Orange County. 

Specifically, I am requesting records containing the following information for the calendar years **2023, 2024, 2025, and 2026 (year-to-date)**:

1. **Placements by Destination Zip Code:** The total number of child welfare placements (including foster family homes, group homes, short-term residential therapeutic programs (STRTPs), and transitional housing) routed to destinations within Orange County, broken down by destination zip code.
2. **Placement Origin Zip Code:** The origin zip codes of the children prior to placement in Orange County facilities, or aggregate counts indicating whether children placed in Orange County facilities originated from within Orange County or from out-of-county jurisdictions.
3. **Facility Type Totals:** The total number of active placements in Orange County facilities during the requested time frame, broken down by facility license type (e.g., STRTP, Foster Family Home) and zip code.

**De-identification and Privacy:**
To protect individual privacy and comply with Welfare and Institutions Code § 10850, please aggregate all data. We do not request, and explicitly request you redact, any personally identifiable information (PII) such as child names, exact birth dates, case numbers, or specific facility names/street addresses if they would identify a specific youth. Under Gov. Code § 7922.500, if any portion of these records is exempt, the remaining non-exempt portions must be segregated and disclosed.

If the records are available in an electronic format (such as Excel, CSV, or PDF), please provide them in that format via email or portal upload.

If you determine that any portion of this request is exempt from disclosure, please specify the specific statutory exemption you are relying upon. Thank you for your assistance.
